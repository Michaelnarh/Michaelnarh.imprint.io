Guidelines

1. To use the imprint Generator, upload the list of webiste in excel sheet with the links as the first Column.

2. Click on the Generate button

3. Wait until the page finishes loading, to download the Text files Click on the Download button

4. After successful download, to upload new website sheet, click on the Delete Files button to remove previous files

5. It is Advised to load 100 list to avoid unending loading of the website., but if you have patience more can be uploaded

5. The download files contains the list of webiste visited successfuly and scraped the imprint content numbered 1 to the last row uploaded

5. Unsuccessful links are skipped in the zip text files
